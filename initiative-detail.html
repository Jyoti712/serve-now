ServeNow Initiatives

Driving change through action. Here’s how we’re making a difference.

Environmental Clean-Up

Organizing clean-ups in public spaces while spreading awareness on recycling and waste management.

Senior Companionship

Volunteers engage with elderly individuals to provide support, conversation, and joy.

Digital Literacy

Teaching digital skills and internet safety to underserved groups, especially elders and women.

Clothing & Essentials

Distributing donated clothes, blankets, and hygiene kits to those in need.

Plantation Drives

Greening our cities through tree planting and urban gardening initiatives.

Animal Shelter Help

Assisting animal shelters by caring for rescued pets and promoting adoption.

Mental Health Awareness

Organizing campaigns and webinars on mental well-being and emotional resilience.

Disaster Relief

Providing emergency aid and fundraising support during floods, fires, and other crises.

Women Empowerment

Offering skill-building programs like sewing, coding, and entrepreneurship for women.
